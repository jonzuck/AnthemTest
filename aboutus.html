Our mission is to improve the lives of the people we serve

With Anthem HealthKeepers Plus offered by HealthKeepers, Inc., we offer managed care services to enrollees of Virginia's FAMIS and Medicaid/FAMIS MOMS programs.

Anthem HealthKeepers Plus is offered statewide! Find your Virginia Regional Open Enrollment.

We are proud to note that HealthKeepers, Inc.:

  • Are the largest Virginia Medicaid managed care organization
  • Have participated in the Medicaid managed care program since it began in 1996
  • Are the only statewide Medicaid managed care program effective July 1, 2013
  • Serve more than 250,000 Virginia Medicaid, FAMIS and FAMIS MOMS enrollees
